## Content Creation Without Setup Creep

Most content tools require endless configuration — API keys, browser setups, webhook configs. Leon's Hermes setup scraped Amazon (a notoriously difficult target) with zero additional config, generated YouTube titles from two angles, and used free local tools for speech and voice.

> *"Successfully scraped Amazon (notoriously difficult) without additional config. Free speech-to-text via local Whisper, free TTS via Edge TTS. YouTube title generator skill produces five search-based, five browse-targeted, and five hybrid titles."*
>
> — **Leon van Zyl** on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jmtpYUOr7_U)

### The Content Kit

| Tool | What It Does | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| Hermes scraping | Amazon product data | $0 |
| Whisper (local) | Speech-to-text | $0 |
| Edge TTS | Text-to-speech | $0 |
| YouTube title skill | 15 titles per video | $0 |

### Title Strategy

The YouTube title skill generates from three distinct angles:

1. **Search-based titles** — What people are actually searching for
2. **Browse-targeted titles** — What's trending on YouTube
3. **Hybrid titles** — Combine both strategies for maximum reach

---

## Steps

1. **Let Hermes scrape** — Don't pre-configure scrapers, just ask
2. **Use built-in Whisper** — Transcribe audio/video locally for free
3. **Use Edge TTS** — Free, high-quality text-to-speech
4. **Build the title skill** — Prompt for search/browse/hybrid variants
5. **Iterate** — Analyze which titles perform and update the skill
