### What Makes This "Personal"?

Most AI interactions are stateless — each conversation starts from scratch. A personal AI with persistent memory is fundamentally different:

- **Knows your preferences** — Tone, format, depth, areas of expertise
- **Tracks projects** — What you're working on, where you left off
- **Learns from mistakes** — Remembers what you corrected and adjusts
- **Builds on past solutions** — Doesn't reinvent the wheel each time
- **Adapts to your style** — The more you use it, the better it fits

### Why Claude Opus via OpenRouter

Using Claude Opus (one of the most capable models) through OpenRouter keeps costs low:

- **Pay per token** — No monthly subscription, just usage
- **Access to multiple models** — Switch between Opus, Sonnet, Haiku as needed
- **No direct API key management** — One OpenRouter key covers everything
- **Fallback routing** — If Opus is down, route to the next best model
