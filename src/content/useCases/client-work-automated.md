### What a 297-Day Streak Actually Means

Ten months of daily operation. Not a single skipped day. At this point you stop wondering if the agent will do the job and start wondering how you managed without it. It knows your clients, your processes, your standards. It knows what needs a human and what doesn't.

### What $100K+ Looks Like in Practice

At this scale, automated client work includes:

- **Content production** — blog posts, newsletters, social media at volume
- **Research and analysis** — competitor monitoring, market trends, research
- **Process automation** — workflows that used to need human hands
- **Client communication** — drafting, summarizing, triaging
- **Data processing** — raw data into business intelligence

Hermes doesn't just replace busywork. It frees up enough time to take on more clients without hiring more people.

## Do This Yourself

1. **Start the streak** — Deploy Hermes somewhere persistent (VPS, home server, even a phone)
2. **Automate one thing at a time** — Pick one recurring task and get it right
3. **Build on success** — Each automated workflow frees you to tackle the next
4. **Track the value** — Measure what you'd bill for the same work manually
5. **Keep iterating** — Review, refine, expand. Production agents improve with use
