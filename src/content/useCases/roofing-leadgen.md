## Building Business Apps Without the Platform Lock-In

When @cyberfarmacist saw a video of someone using AI to "vibe code" an app on Replit, they wondered if they could do the same — but for a real client: a friend who owns a remodeling company. The result was a roofing lead-gen app built with Hermes, saving significant costs compared to Replit's subscription model.

> *"One of the apps I'm working on helps my friend who owns a remodeling company find work. Specifically roofing work. It started after I saw a video of a guy using ChatGPT and Replit to vibe code some app. I just wondered if I could do it. But the cost of Replit is killing me and the project isn't even a full on CRM lead gen product."*
>
> — **@cyberfarmacist** on Discord

---

### The Problem: Replit Costs

Replit is a popular cloud coding platform, but its costs add up quickly:

| Replit Plan | Monthly Cost | Limitations |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| Hacker | $25/mo | Limited compute, no team features |
| Pro | $50/mo | Better compute, still limited |
| Teams | $100+/mo | For teams, overkill for solo dev |

For a single app that's still in development, these costs are hard to justify. Especially when the app itself is a relatively straightforward CRM/lead-gen tool.

### The Hermes Solution

By switching to Hermes, the developer gained:

- **Zero platform fees** — Hermes is free, open-source software
- **Full control** — The entire codebase lives on your own server
- **Unlimited compute** — Only limited by your VPS resources
- **Persistent development context** — Hermes remembers the project state
- **AI-assisted coding** — Hermes helps write, debug, and refactor
- **Self-hosted deployment** — No per-seat licensing

### What the App Does

The roofing lead-gen app includes:

- **Lead capture** — Website form integration for service requests
- **CRM functionality** — Track leads through the sales pipeline
- **Job scheduling** — Assign crews to roofing projects
- **Estimate generation** — Automated quotes based on roof measurements
- **Customer follow-up** — Automated email/SMS sequences
- **Analytics dashboard** — See conversion rates and revenue

### The Development Process with Hermes

Instead of wrestling with Replit's environment, the developer:

1. **Described the app** in natural language to Hermes
2. **Hermes generated the initial codebase** — frontend, backend, database
3. **Iterated through conversation** — "Add a lead scoring system" → code changed
4. **Deployed on own VPS** — No platform lock-in
5. **Continued development** — Hermes remembers the full project context
